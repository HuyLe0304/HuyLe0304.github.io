Highlights of the Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra


Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra detailed review


The Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra is a phone that will change the future of smartphone photography . Discover the historic step forward with 5 super-sharp 108MP camera lenses, cinematic 8K movie recording on the S20 Ultra.


Enjoy the large, cinematic 6.9-inch screen


Immerse yourself in the action that comes to life on the large 6.9-inch Infinity-O display on the Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra. The display with premium Dynamic AMOLED technology, HDR10 + support and ultra-sharp QuadHD + resolution, makes every picture look unprecedented with impressive contrast and color.


120Hz screen, smooth in every gesture


Samsung S20 Ultra owns a screen with a refresh rate of 120Hz, double that of other screens on the market. Thanks to the high frequency of scanning, everything responds more quickly and smoothly. Your gaming experience is also enhanced as the frame rate surpasses the standard 60fps threshold for impeccable smoothness and precision in battle.


Premium and durable


In addition to the premium design, showing class and luxury in your hands, the Galaxy S20 Ultra also has incredible durability. The machine is made of toughened glass and metal, more durable when a shock occurs. On top of that, the S20 Ultra is IP68 waterproof , can be immersed in 1.5m water for 30 minutes, giving you peace of mind against any dangers from water.


Camera changed the future of photography


Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra features an unprecedented camera cluster on a smartphone. The phone has 4 rear cameras, in which the main camera has a resolution of up to 108MP, and the telephoto camera has a resolution of 48MP. The hardware superiority gives the S20 Ultra the pinnacle in photography, with incredibly sharp images and movies.


Ability to enlarge photos up to 100 times


Believe it or not, you can magnify images on the S20 Ultra up to 100x and still have great quality. The Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra's camera lens goes beyond the usual 12MP resolution when it reaches 108MP, combined with the 48MP Telephoto lens to bring 4x optical zoom, 10x hybrid zoom and digital zoom up to 100x. Get ready to break any distance, bring distant objects in sight, for incredibly sharp and detailed images.


Challenge the night, create masterpieces


With an excellent camera system, Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra is not afraid of low light conditions. In contrast, the shimmering lights in the night become true works of art when captured by the S20 Ultra camera. Night Mode will take multiple photos at the same time, stitch them together and give the best shot with great detail, great noise reduction, brightness and contrast.


Shine with confidence with the 40MP selffie camera


Not only the rear camera, the front camera of the Galaxy S20 Ultra also has the highest resolution on the market today. Sensors up to 40MP with the most advanced technology, S20 Ultra will help you capture sharp selfies, highlighting natural beauty in all situations, including low light.


8K movie shooting, a real step forward in the smartphone industry


The superior hardware system allows Galaxy S20 Ultra to become the first phone that can record 8K quality movies, 4 times sharper than standard 4K and 16 times sharper than Full HD. Try to show super sharp movies on 8K TV or even big screen like the cinema, you will see the incredible quality that the S20 Ultra brings. In addition, you can also crop photos with impressive moments in those videos to get a photo with up to 33MP resolution, clear to every detail.


Super anti-shake video, capture the moment


Fast-moving videos are all recorded on Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra in the smoothest way, images are not blurred. The combination of a large image sensor, AI super-stabilization and OIS optical image stabilization gives you quality movies, capturing all the memorable moments.


The power of the future


The configuration that Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra is equipped with is so powerful that it breaks all previous limits, giving you a great experience. The Exynos 990 processor manufactured on the 7nm + process comes with 12GB of RAM, which is about the same power as desktop models. You can also use your phone as an authentic calculator through the Samsung Dex feature. Memory is something you do not need to think about on the S20 Ultra as it has 128GB of built-in memory and can support 1TB more via the microSD card slot. You will always have enough storage space for essential data.


5000 mAh battery capacity, confidently towards the top


The large capacity 5000 mAh Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra battery is ready for the ultimate experience. Enjoy all-day use from just one charge. Moreover, the phone also supports 45W super fast charging; Super speed 2.0 wireless charging and wireless charging sharing. Only a few minutes of charging to add hours of experience, the S20 Ultra understands your active life.


Advanced security


Integrated Samsung Knox security platform, Galaxy S20 Ultra has multiple layers of protection on the phone to minimize the risk of data theft. Besides, you can unlock the device with the most advanced security methods available today, which is the ultrasonic fingerprint sensor right on the screen or facial recognition.
